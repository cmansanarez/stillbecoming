stillbecoming

This is stillbecoming.

A generative ritual offered in celebration of your life.
Not to mark a destination, but to honor the arc you are moving through.

Your life, like sacred geometry, is shaped by repetition, deviation, and quiet moments of transformation. Order gives way to noise. Noise finds its way back to form.

What you witness here is a brief performance.
What remains is a relic of that becoming.

May this piece remind you that growth is not linear,
and that you are always in the act of becoming more yourself.

You're welcome to continue here,
but this piece is best experienced on a larger screen.

The ritual unfolds slowly, with details and depth that benefit from space.
If you're able, we invite you to return on a desktop or laptop
and witness the full becoming.

Whether now or later,
the moment will be waiting.
